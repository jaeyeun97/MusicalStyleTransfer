Section 1

Here you will be presented with 30 pairs of sound clips. We ask you to listen to and compare the two sound clips, and answer the following two questions:

  • Which of the two clips more chilled?
  • How similar do the two clips sound?

For the first question you will be given two choices, and you can choose the one that sounds more chilled to your taste. For the latter, you will be given a Likert scale, from 'Very Different' to 'Very Simliar'; please choose the option that describes the two clips best. You can listen to both clips as many times as you require to make your choice.

To give you a better sense of a chill song, we have prepared samples from a curated playlist of chill music. (Many thanks to MellowBeatSeaker; please go subscribe on his channel!) We recommend you to take a listen to the clips below to aid your judgement of chillness.
